Mohinga

Description

Mohinga is a delicious myanmar tradional breakfast. Burmese consumes Mohinga as their primary breakfast.
Even though, Mohinga has amount of oil, it really suitable for burmese who lives in burma and needs a specific amount of oil according to their living style.

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Ginger, garlic cloves, red chillies, turmeric powder, onions, oil
  • Toasted rice powder
  • banana stem, sliced essential

Steps:

  1. Prepare the fish and make the onion paste.
  2. Step 1: Prepare the lemongrass.
  3. Step 2: Saute the lemongrass, onion, ginger, and garlic in coconut oil.
  4. Step 3: Add the turmeric and roasted chilli powder.
  5. Step 4: Add the fisth broth.
  6. Step 5: Add the fish.
  7. Step 6: ADD rice noodle and season to taste.

Now you can consume delicious Mohinga and serves with Top-ons you prefer.
