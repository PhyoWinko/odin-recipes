Shwe Gyi

Description

Shwe Gyi Mont is one of the most popular burmese dessert. You can eat it as breakfast or have it after meal deliciously.

Ingredients

  1. eggs, milk, sugar, poppy seeds, butter
  2. Semolina

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Add roasted semolina to the pan and keep stirring. In a tray brush a littl ebutter all over and add the selimona mixture.
  2. Step 2: Bake it at 145-163 degree celcius.

Once it done, cut the cakke and add your preferred ones for more taste.
